## Concept

Story Weaver addresses one of the core challenges of AI creative writing: **narrative coherence across long sessions**. Most LLMs forget plot details after a few thousand tokens. Story Weaver maintains a structured story bible:

- Character profiles (traits, motivations, relationships)
- Timeline of events
- Unresolved plot threads
- World rules and lore

## Planned Features

- **Branching paths** — the agent proposes 2–3 narrative directions at key story moments
- **Consistency checks** — flags contradictions against the story bible before generating new scenes
- **Export** — renders the full story as a formatted PDF or ebook

## Status

Currently designing the memory schema. The tricky bit is deciding what's worth persisting vs. what the LLM can derive from context on demand.
